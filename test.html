Instructions

  • Keep separate containers for dry and wet waste in the kitchen
  • Keep two bags for dry waste collection - one for paper and plastic ,and other for the rest of the household waste
  • Keep plastics from the kitchen clean and dry and drop into the dry waste bin
  • Send wet waste out of the home daily and send dry waste out of the home, once a week
